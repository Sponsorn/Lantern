## LuckyWipeTools 
Collection of raid tools built for the Lucky Wipe guild. All features can be enabled/disabled individually.

#### Gateway Alert
*   Persistent on-screen alert when your Demonic Gateway Shard is ready to use.
*   Auto-hides when the item goes on cooldown.
*   Optional combat-only mode.

#### Summon Helper
*   Tracks summoning portal placement and summon accept/decline status.
*   Shows a roster of raid members outside your zone with class-colored names.
*   Click a name to target that player.
*   On-screen alerts for portal placed, summon accepted, and summon declined.

#### Vantus Rune Distribution
*   Players type `/lwt vantus` to request a vantus rune.
*   Distributors see a roster of players needing runes — requested players highlighted, missing-buff players auto-detected.
*   Left-click to initiate trade, right-click to remove from list.
*   Auto-removes players when trade completes or vantus buff is detected.
*   Clears on boss kill. Configurable for Heroic and Mythic only.
*   Requires LuckyWipeTools on both sender and receiver (hidden addon messages).

#### Combat Log
*   Automatically starts and stops combat logging based on instance type and difficulty.
*   Configurable per instance type (raids, dungeons, arena, battlegrounds, scenarios) and difficulty.
*   Defaults to Heroic Raid, Mythic Raid, and Mythic+.

#### Item Splitter
*   Split item stacks into smaller stacks for distribution.
*   Works with personal bags and guild bank.
*   Quantity limiter — split a specific number of stacks instead of all.
*   "Split to Bags" / "Split in Bank" buttons when guild bank is open.
*   A Split button also appears on the guild bank frame.
*   `/lwt split` to open the splitter popup.

#### Display Settings

Each alert feature (Gateway, Summon Helper, Vantus) has independent display settings:
*   Font, font size, and font color.
*   Sound selection (supports LibSharedMedia).
*   Adjustable position with unlock/lock mover.
*   Duration (for timed alerts).

#### Configuration

Type `/lwt` to open the settings panel. Each feature has its own page with an enable toggle and description.
