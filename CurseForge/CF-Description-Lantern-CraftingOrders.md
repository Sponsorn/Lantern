Lantern: Crafting Orders adds lightweight, reliable notifications for crafting orders.

Everything is possible to turn off.

*   Guild orders: announces placement and fulfillment in guild chat.
*   Personal orders: on-screen notification when you receive a new personal crafting order, with customizable font, size, outline, color, and duration. Optional sound alert.
*   Quality of life: optional "Complete + Whisper" button to finish a personal order and auto-whisper the customer with a custom message.

Designed to be small, robust, and compatible with the default UI.
